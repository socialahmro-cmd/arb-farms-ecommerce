Why Consume Chia Seeds Daily?

Chia seeds absorb up to 10-12 times their weight in water, expanding in your stomach to prolong satiety, suppress hunger cravings, and slow glucose release. They are packed with soluble dietary fibers that feed beneficial gut microbiomes.

Classic Healthy Recipe: Chia Seed Pudding

Whisk 2 tablespoons of ARB Chia Seeds into 1/2 cup of almond or cow milk. Let it stand for 5 minutes, stir to prevent clumping, then cover and refrigerate overnight. Top with honey or fresh fruit slice before serving.
