Daily Intake Guide

Introduce corn silage gradually to your dairy livestock over a 7-day period. For adult dairy cows and buffaloes, feed approximately 15 to 25 kg of silage per day, combined with dry wheat straw or green fodder.

Keep bales wrapped until feeding. Once a bale is opened, consume within 5 days to prevent aerobic deterioration and nutrient losses.
