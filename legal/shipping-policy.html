Shipping & Delivery Policy

Last updated: May 19, 2026

1. Scope & Territories

ARB Farms delivers agricultural seeds, organic foods, and feed products nationwide across Pakistan. We utilize specialized third-party logistics agents (TCS, Leopards, and Trax Courier) to fulfill all domestic orders.

2. Dairy Shipment Restriction (Multan Only)

Due to strict refrigeration and shelf-life requirements, fresh pasteurized items listed under our Dairy catalog (specifically Cow Milk, Buffalo Milk, and Goat Milk) are eligible for delivery **exclusively within the municipal boundaries of Multan city**. Checkout operations attempting to schedule fresh dairy delivery outside Multan will be automatically restricted.

3. Weight-Based Shipping Tiers

To maintain fair pricing across agricultural feed shipments, grains, and small organic product packages, shipping charges are calculated dynamically. Wheat products have a custom shipping rate structure due to bulk packaging requirements:

Standard Products (All Non-Wheat Items)

Order Weight Range Rate Structure
1 gm to 1000 gm (1.0 Kg) Rs. 300 flat rate
1001 gm to 5999 gm (1.001 Kg to 5.999 Kg) Rs. 300 per Kg (Rounded up to nearest whole Kg)
6000 gm (6.0 Kg) and above Rs. 150 per Kg (Rounded up to nearest whole Kg)

Wheat Products

Order Weight Range Rate Structure
0.5 gm to 5999 gm (Up to 5.999 Kg) Rs. 300 per Kg (Rounded up to nearest whole Kg)
6000 gm to 40000 gm (6.0 Kg to 40.0 Kg) Rs. 150 per Kg (Rounded up to nearest whole Kg)
40000 gm (40.0 Kg / 1 Maund) and above Rs. 1,500 per Maund (40 Kg units, fractions round up)

4. Delivery Timelines

Standard delivery cycles depend on regional zones:

  • Punjab & Federal: 2 to 3 business days.
  • Sindh, KPK, & Balochistan: 3 to 5 business days.

5. 100% Advance Bank Transfer

Orders require a 50% advance payment via EasyPaisa, JazzCash, or bank transfer, with the remaining 50% paid upon delivery. Customers must prepare the remaining 50% physical cash ready to hand over to the courier representative at the time of delivery.
