Refund & Return Policy

Last updated: May 19, 2026

1. Return Window

ARB Farms accepts return requests for eligible products within **7 days** of package receipt. Requests submitted beyond this window cannot be accommodated.

2. Dairy & Perishable Exclusions

For public health and food safety reasons, fresh dairy items (specifically Cow Milk, Buffalo Milk, and Goat Milk) are **exempt from returns or refunds** once dispatched. If you receive spoiled milk due to courier delays, contact support immediately at our WhatsApp hotline for a direct replacement dispatch or credit.

3. Seed Purity & Germination Claims

We guarantee high-germination rates (exceeding 90%) for our sowing crop seeds under correct soil and watering practices. If you observe germination failures:

  • Please document the sowing process and soil parameters.
  • Submit photos of the crop patch within 14 days of planting.
  • We will review and issue replacement seed batches for validated claims.

4. Packaging Seals

Edible seeds, Desi Ghee, Honey, and Achaar jars are only eligible for refund if they are returned **unopened, with the original security seals intact** and packaging undamaged.

5. Return Shipping

Customers are responsible for booking return courier shipments to our main hub in Multan. Original shipping fees are non-refundable. Refund settlements are processed via EasyPaisa or bank transfer within 5 business days after our farm quality check team inspects the returned package.
