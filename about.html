Our Heritage

Off-Grid Farming, Pure Quality Staples

Discover the ARB Farms philosophy: raising agricultural standards, building soil nutrition naturally, and delivering premium chemical-free food from Multan to your table.

Born out of Passion for Sustainable Agriculture

Located at Street 1, Fatima Avenue, Opp. Fatima Masjid, Near Prime Mart. MPS Road in Multan, Punjab, ARB Farms was established with a singular mission: to transition traditional agricultural practices into sustainable, solar-powered, off-grid systems that enrich the environment instead of depleting it.

Unlike commercial agricultural enterprises that rely heavily on chemical fertilizers, synthetic growth boosters, and GMO crops, we utilize composted cattle manure, organic vermicompost, and heirloom non-hybrid crop variants. This ensures that every seed sold holds high germination potential and every food product retains its traditional nutritious profile.

Our Three Pillars of Integrity

Sustainable & Off-Grid

Our tube wells and processing machineries are entirely driven by custom-built solar grids. We operate zero diesel engines, offsetting carbon footprints directly at the source.

Chemical Purity Guaranteed

Our Desi Ghee is slow-cooked over wood fire from milk collected from open-pasture cows. No hormonal injections are administered, resulting in rich beta-carotene fats.

Purity Tested Seed Batches

We supply local farmers with high germination rate seeds, tested in agricultural laboratories for weed separation, purity, and disease resistance.
